# Day 25 — AI Shark Tank Simulator

> **Challenge:** Build an interactive AI Shark Tank Simulator that allows founders to pitch startup ideas, answer investor questions, receive an investment scorecard, and download a professional pitch report.

---

## 📌 Objective

The objective of Day 25 was to create a complete startup investment simulation where users can:

- Submit a startup pitch
- Face AI-generated investor questions
- Defend their business idea
- Receive investment feedback
- Analyze strengths and weaknesses
- Download a professional pitch report

---

## 🚀 What I Built

I developed an **AI Shark Tank Simulator** as a single-page HTML application.

The simulator includes:

- Startup pitch form
- Interactive investor Q&A
- AI-generated scorecard
- Investment verdict
- Downloadable pitch report
- Leaderboard support
- Modern responsive UI

---

## 💡 Startup Idea Used

### SipSwift

An on-demand alcohol delivery platform that connects customers with nearby licensed liquor stores.

Instead of maintaining inventory, SipSwift purchases alcohol from the customer's selected licensed store and delivers it to their doorstep while following legal age verification and local regulations.

Additional offerings include:

- Ice
- Mixers
- Soft drinks
- Party snacks
- Disposable party essentials

## 📚 Key Learnings

- A strong startup idea alone is not enough.
- Investors expect clear market validation.
- Unit economics must be realistic and defensible.
- Execution plans matter as much as innovation.
- Legal and operational risks significantly affect investment decisions.
- Presenting concise, data-backed answers improves investor confidence.
