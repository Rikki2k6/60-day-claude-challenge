# Day 16 – Custom Claude Skill: Stock Fundamental Research

## Overview
Day 16 focused on creating and testing a reusable Claude Custom Skill for stock fundamental analysis. The objective was to build a skill that can generate professional stock research reports without needing to paste long prompts every time.

---

## Task Completed

### 1. Created Custom Skill
**Skill Name:** `stock-fundamental-research`

The custom skill was configured with detailed instructions for analyzing stocks using publicly available financial data, valuation metrics, ownership patterns, growth trends, and risk factors.

### 2. Tested the Skill
Generated a complete fundamental research report for:

**Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)**

The report included:

- Company Overview
- Financial Performance Analysis
- Revenue & Profit Growth Trends
- Valuation Metrics
- ROE and ROCE Analysis
- Shareholding Pattern Analysis
- Risk Factors
- Key Growth Drivers
- Quarterly Performance Indicators
- Investment Thesis
- Key Metrics to Watch
- Final Research Summary

---

## Key Observations

### Strengths of the Skill
- Produces structured institutional-style reports.
- Generates valuation and growth analysis automatically.
- Includes business risks and opportunities.
- Creates reusable workflows without re-entering prompts.
- Suitable for comparing multiple stocks.

### Example Metrics Analyzed for TCS
- Market Capitalization
- P/E Ratio
- Revenue Growth
- Net Profit Growth
- ROE
- ROCE
- Dividend Yield
- Free Cash Flow
- AI Revenue Trends
- Shareholding Changes

---

## Key Learnings

### Financial Analysis Concepts Learned
- Difference between Revenue Growth and Profit Growth.
- Importance of ROE and ROCE.
- Impact of valuation multiples on investment decisions.
- Understanding revenue concentration risks.
- Tracking institutional ownership trends.
- Evaluating long-term growth potential.

### Skill Reusability
The biggest advantage of Claude Skills is that once the skill is created, it can be reused across conversations without repeatedly providing the full prompt and instructions.
